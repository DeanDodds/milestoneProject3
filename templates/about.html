About us

Recipe app originally started as a simple idea in 2020 between partners Anna & Marcus. they recognised the need for tried and tested recipes for new cooks to try. To share their recipes with friends and family, they created The recipe app as a tool for posting recipes and helpful information in the hopes that others would join in and create a community.
